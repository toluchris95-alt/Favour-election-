About Oluwagbuyi Favour (SKYTUNEZ)

A dedicated 200L Medicine & Surgery student with a passion for service, wellness, and effective student advocacy.

Who He Is

Favour is a proactive member of the Excelsiors class who believes leadership begins with listening and serving. From volunteering in health outreaches to coordinating study groups, he has consistently put his classmates first.

He’s stepping up to serve, not just to represent — ensuring every voice is heard, every opinion is respected, and every concern is addressed with action.

Why He’s Running

To help our 90+ classmates thrive — academically, socially, and personally. The goal is a class culture where everyone feels supported, informed, and empowered to succeed.

“Let’s make our class stronger, more connected, and united in our goals. Let’s move forward — together.”

His Platform: A Pact with the Class

Practical, empathetic, and results-driven promises designed for medical students.

Better Study Resources

Centralize access to lecture slides, past questions, and lab resources through a shared, organized drive.
